Clauses
1. Clause 1: Pre-existing conditions are excluded from coverage for the first 12 months of the policy.
2. Clause 2: Coverage for experimental or investigational treatments is not provided under this policy.
3. Clause 3: Emergency room visits require prior authorization except in cases of life-threatening emergenc
4. Clause 4: Out-of-network providers are covered at 60% after a $2,000 deductible.
5. Clause 5: Prescription drugs are covered at 80% after the deductible is met.
6. Clause 6: Preventive care services are fully covered with no deductible.
